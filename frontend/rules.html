Rules & Regulations

  1. Ragging is Strictly Banned: Ragging is a cognizable offense. Any student involved will face immediate action, including expulsion from the hostel.
  2. Attendance is Mandatory: All students must use the IN/OUT entry register and biometric system. A minimum of 90% attendance in all classes is required.
  3. Room Allotment & Conduct: Rooms are allotted by the Warden and cannot be changed between semesters. Students are responsible for keeping their rooms neat and must switch off lights and fans when leaving.
  4. No Unauthorized Guests: Day scholars, parents, or any unauthorized persons are strictly prohibited from entering hostel rooms.
  5. Personal Valuables: Students are responsible for their own belongings like laptops, mobiles, and money. The hostel management is not responsible for any loss.
  6. Prohibited Activities: Consumption of alcohol, smoking, playing loud music, and posting on walls are strictly prohibited. Violators will be expelled immediately.
  7. Mess Hall Discipline: Students must follow the queue system in the dining hall. Wasting food is not advised, and carrying food to rooms is strictly forbidden.
  8. Hostel Leave & ID Cards: Prior written permission from the Warden is required for leaving the hostel for holidays. Students must carry their Hostel ID card at all times.
